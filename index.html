The Pathway

The Innovation Journey

Every girl moves through four transformative stages — from curiosity to career, from student to entrepreneur.

01

Masterclass

Girls are introduced to design thinking, creative ideation, and professional mindset. Sessions are delivered in schools by trained facilitators and industry guests.

02

Diamond Club

Top participants are invited into Diamond Clubs — peer networks anchored in schools, fueled by monthly guest sessions, mentorship circles, and collaborative challenges.

03

Internship

Graduates from our bootcamps access a structured internship pipeline — placing them with partner organizations for real-world, paid experience.

04

Economic Independence

Through The Diamond Agency, graduates access clients, earn income, and build careers. This is the destination: self-sustaining, economically empowered young women.

Program Detail

The School Series

3Sprint
Phases

Design Thinking Sprint

Our flagship school program takes girls through a structured journey of human-centered design — teaching them to understand, create, and present real solutions to real problems.

①
Empathize

Girls identify a real community problem. They interview, listen, and build empathy for those they aim to serve.

②
Ideate

Using design frameworks, girls brainstorm and prototype creative solutions in collaborative teams.

③
Prototype & Present

Teams build and present their ideas at Grand Demo Day — in front of mentors, community members, and industry leaders.

Grand Demo Day

The culminating event of every school sprint. Girls present their innovations to a live audience of mentors, community leaders, and industry professionals — building confidence, public speaking, and visibility.

Community & Mentorship

Diamond Clubs

Diamond Clubs are our in-school peer networks — structured communities that keep girls connected, motivated, and growing between formal programs. Each club is anchored by a trained facilitator and meets monthly.

  • ◆
    Guest Sessions — industry professionals share their journeys and answer questions directly
  • ◆
    Mentorship Circles — small-group guidance from women ahead on the path
  • ◆
    Challenges & Showcases — friendly competitions that build skill and visibility

The Vision Ahead

Our Strategic Roadmap

We are building in three deliberate phases — from school-level activation to a national ecosystem of economically independent young women.

01
Phase One

Activation

Enter schools. Launch Diamond Clubs. Run Design Thinking Sprints. Build the community of girls who believe they can.

  • 18+ schools in target communities
  • Design Thinking curriculum deployed
  • First cohort of Diamond Club members
02
Phase Two

Network

Connect schools. Launch bootcamps. Build the internship pipeline. Activate The Diamond Agency.

  • Multi-state program expansion
  • Bootcamp cohorts across skill tracks
  • First paying clients for the Agency
03
Phase Three

Ecosystem

Scale nationally. Build the alumni network. Establish Diamond Mind as Africa's foremost institute for youth economic independence.

  • 1,040 girls impacted by 2027
  • National alumni and mentor network
  • Fully self-sustaining social enterprise
